Join Us! Building better business

Level Up Connections is an evolving business development program to create a focused, supportive community of business professionals where all ages, industries, and levels of experience join together in a shared desire to be better and do more. By expanding your business network and building up your contact-base for referrals, you will see results in your business development and growth.

Why do you and your team need to Level Up?

  • As business owners, managers, or entrepreneurs we all face similar challenges in one way or another. The topics are designed to touch all aspects of business. From marketing, networking, brand recognition, cash flow, legal, and even simple hard topics like hiring and firing! Level Up Connect will help build not only our network but your business knowledge as well!
  • People refer business to people they like and trust! Level Up Connections will facilitate the connections between members and encouraged to connect each-other with referral partners. The connections you can make are endless! Level Up Member Mindset To help each other Level Up in all aspects of business, and to make connections for a lifetime!

Looking For a Chapter?

Your first step is to fill out application so we can connect you to a chapter near you or a chapter missing your expertise! Registration is simple, fill out an application, once approved and chapter is chosen have a 1-1 with chapter president and attend 2 meetings with no commitment! The beginning to Level Up your Connection!

We welcome our guests as if they were members already! Short introductions by everyone, followed by the meetings, planned agenda topics, and group discussions to help narrow down individual problems, concerns, or experience in the topics! Helping each-other Level Up! Creating value, knowledge, and setting goals is essential in our meetings!
